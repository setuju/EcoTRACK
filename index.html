How EcoTrack Works

1. Enter your lifestyle data – Fill in the form with your weekly/monthly habits.

2. Choose your country – Electricity emissions vary by location.

3. Click "Calculate" – Get your annual carbon footprint and personalized recommendations.

4. Save your results – Track progress over time (stored locally or anonymously in the cloud).

Methodology: Emission factors from EPA, IPCC, and Poore & Nemecek (2018). Grid intensity from Electricity Maps.
